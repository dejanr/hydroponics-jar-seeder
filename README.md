# hydroponics jar seeder

Hydroponic jar seeder sized for a jar opening of 74 mm.

## Files

- `jar_seeder.scad` — seeder cup with dense bottom hole pattern
- `yogurt_tub_seeder.scad` — 120 mm yogurt tub seeder with shallow cup, denser holes, and angled material-saving no-support flange
- `jar_seeder_lid.scad` — flat light-blocking germination lid with hollow ribbed inset
- `dist/jar_seeder_74mm_x30.stl` — final seeder STL
- `dist/yogurt_tub_seeder_120mm_x15_s3p5.stl` — yogurt tub seeder STL
- `dist/jar_seeder_lid_74mm.stl` — final lid STL
- `previews/` — rendered preview images

## Default model sizes

### Seeder
- Jar opening: 74 mm
- Depth: 30 mm
- Wall thickness: 3 mm
- Bottom thickness: 3 mm

### Yogurt tub seeder
- Tub opening: 120 mm
- Depth: 15 mm
- Bottom hole spacing: 3.5 mm
- Angled flange underside for support-free printing
- Flared inner flange to reduce material

### Lid
- Flat top thickness: 2.4 mm
- Hollow inset depth: 5 mm
- Internal support ribs enabled
